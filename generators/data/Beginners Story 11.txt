Beginner's Story 11 — Building a Snowman: Tim's Wintry Friend

Segment 1  
Spoken: As fresh snow covers the ground, Tim feels inspired to embark on a frosty construction project.  
Code: Tim starts a snowball.

Segment 2  
Spoken: He rolls a small snowball around the yard, watching it grow bigger with each turn.  
Code: The snowball gets big.

Segment 3  
Spoken: With effort, Tim completes the base of his creation, laying the foundation for his snowy companion.  
Code: Tim places the snowball.

Segment 4  
Spoken: A second, smaller globe of snow forms the torso, carefully lifted atop the first.  
Code: Tim adds a second ball.

Segment 5  
Spoken: The smallest snowball, soon to be the head, crowns the emerging figure with care.  
Code: Tim adds the head.

Segment 6  
Spoken: Tim steps back to plan the face, deciding on a cheerful expression for his snowman.  
Code: Tim plans the snowman face.

Segment 7  
Spoken: For the nose, a bright carrot is chosen, adding a pop of color against the white snow.  
Code: Tim inserts carrot nose.

Segment 8  
Spoken: Coal pieces, found in a small bag, become the eyes, bringing the snowman’s gaze to life.  
Code: Tim places coal eyes.

Segment 9  
Spoken: A smile forms from more coal, ensuring the snowman appears friendly to all who pass by.  
Code: Tim creates a coal smile.

Segment 10  
Spoken: Tim finds sticks nearby, perfect for arms, which he inserts with a sense of finality.  
Code: Tim adds stick arms.

Segment 11  
Spoken: Remembering an old scarf, Tim wraps it around the snowman’s neck, offering warmth.  
Code: Tim ties on a scarf.

Segment 12  
Spoken: An old, worn hat is the final touch, proudly placed atop the snowman’s head.  
Code: Tim puts on a hat.

Segment 13  
Spoken: To make the scene even more picturesque, Tim scatters some birdseed around, inviting wildlife.  
Code: Tim scatters seeds.

Segment 14  
Spoken: With his brother’s help, they give the snowman a broom, adding character to the snowy figure.  
Code: Tim places the broom.

Segment 15  
Spoken: They step back, viewing their work, debating on a suitable name for their friend.  
Code: Tim names the snowman.

Segment 16  
Spoken: Satisfied, they take photos to capture the memory of the day spent in laughter and creation.  
Code: Tim takes a photo.

Segment 17  
Spoken: As evening approaches, they decorate around the snowman with colorful, waterproof lights.  
Code: Tim adds lights.

Segment 18  
Spoken: Hot chocolate in hand, they admire their snowman under the early night sky, aglow with lights.  
Code: They enjoy hot cocoa.

Segment 19  
Spoken: Neighbors pass by, complimenting their handiwork and the joy it brings to the street.  
Code: They get compliments.

Segment 20  
Spoken: With the day winding down, Tim feels a sense of accomplishment and joy at their wintry creation.  
Code: Tim feels happy.
