Segment 1

Code: Deb waters the garden.

Code: Deb finds a nest.

Code: Deb draws nature.

Code: Deb finds a worm.

Code: Deb sees a squirrel.

Spoken: She creates a small rock path leading to her favorite part of the garden, a quiet spot surrounded by flowers.

Code: Deb makes bird sounds.

Code: Deb makes a necklace.

Code: Deb plans a scrapbook.
