Beginner's Story 17 - The Magic Show: Roy's Enchanting Evening

Segment 1  
Spoken: The lights dim in the theater as Roy feels a buzz of excitement for the show about to start.  
Code: The lights dim.

Segment 2  
Spoken: With a dramatic flair, the magician appears on stage, greeted by enthusiastic applause.  
Code: He appears.

Segment 3  
Spoken: For the first act, a bird cage in hand, the magician covers it with a cloth, then poof, it's gone.  
Code: The cage disappears.

Segment 4  
Spoken: The crowd gasps as the magician invites a volunteer for the next trick, a mysterious card game.  
Code: He picks a volunteer.

Segment 5  
Spoken: Roy watches in awe as the magician guesses the volunteer's selected card, each time, without fail.  
Code: He guesses the card.

Segment 6  
Spoken: Next, a series of ropes become entangled and then miraculously untangle at the magician's command.  
Code: The ropes untangle.

Segment 7  
Spoken: For a moment, the magician seems to levitate, hovering above the stage to everyone's amazement.  
Code: He levitates.

Segment 8  
Spoken: A special trick involves disappearing coins that somehow end up in the most unexpected places.  
Code: Coins disappear.

Segment 9  
Spoken: With a wave and a chant, the magician conjures a flurry of flowers from an empty hat.  
Code: Flowers from hat.

Segment 10  
Spoken: The final act promises to be unforgettable, with a suspenseful build up that captivates Roy.  
Code: He prepares the final act.

Segment 11  
Spoken: The magician steps into a cabinet, the door closes, and after a dramatic pause, they have vanished.
Code: He vanishes.

Segment 12  
Spoken: Moments later, to the audience's shock, the magician reappears at the back of the theater.  
Code: He reappears.

Segment 13  
Spoken: Thundering applause fills the room as the magician takes a bow, the show concluding on a high note.  
Code: He takes a bow.

Segment 14  
Spoken: Afterwards, Roy eagerly joins the line to meet the magician, a blend of curiosity and admiration in his heart.  
Code: Roy joins the line.

Segment 15  
Spoken: Approaching the magician, Roy is greeted with a warm smile and a hint of mystery still in the air.  
Code: Roy meets the magician.

Segment 16  
Spoken: Roy asks about the secrets behind the illusions, but the magician winks, saying magic never tells.  
Code: Roy asks about the secrets.

Segment 17  
Spoken: Despite not learning the tricks, Roy feels inspired and thrilled to have witnessed such artistry.  
Code: Roy feels inspired.

Segment 18  
Spoken: Roy leaves the theater with a souvenir program, a tangible memory of the magical evening.  
Code: Roy takes a program.

Segment 19  
Spoken: On the way home, Roy can't stop thinking about the show, replaying each act in his mind.  
Code: Roy thinks about the show.

Segment 20  
Spoken: Settling into bed, Roy dreams of magic and mystery, the wonders of the show lingering in his dreams.  
Code: Roy dreams of magic.
